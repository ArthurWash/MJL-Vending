Hello my name is Mark Lepore, owner and operator of MJL amenities & vending. The main objective of my business is to provide amenities to businesses and properties with my AI smart machines. The machines I use are the future of vending services and comes with many features.

- These machines have a nice appearance and have wheels for easy movement if there was ever a desire to move the machine elsewhere on the property.

- The machine is cashless with a card reader installed. It accepts all credit and debit cards including Apple Pay and Google Pay.

- Unlike the old style vending machines, they don’t have the vending coils that disperse the product, which at times the product gets stuck or the coils don’t work.

- Once the consumer taps or swipes their card, the door unlocks and they can freely grab whatever and however much they would like with one transaction. After each transaction, a QR code appears on the scanner that can be scanned if the consumer would like a receipt for their purchase.

- I have complete control of each machine using the AI Vending App on my smart phone. I would know right away if a machine needs to be restocked with specific products

I also have the convenience to monitor every transaction using the AI sensing cameras built in the machine. If there is an issue with a product that a customer purchased such as being defective or unsealed, they can contact my direct line that is advertised on the side of the machine and inform me of the issue. After reviewing the issue, I can immediately refund the customer using the app. This is a reliable feature that prevents any burden on the property staff.

The advanced technology with these machines provide a pleasant experience for everyone involved. he efficiency that comes with these machines is the reason why I only use these machines. I am 100% responsible for maintaining and stocking the machines at no cost to management. I hope this information provides a great understanding of my services and transpires into an easy decision to have me provide amenities to this property.
